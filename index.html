About Us

Built on Craftsmanship, Driven by Vision

Founded in 2021, Construction Pioneers was built from a simple belief — that every home deserves exceptional craftsmanship, and every homeowner deserves a remodeling experience that is collaborative, transparent, and stress-free.

While our company officially opened its doors in 2021, our roots run much deeper. With over 15 years of hands-on experience in the construction industry, we bring a level of skill, knowledge, and reliability that only comes from years spent perfecting the trade. From rough framing to finish trim, from laying subfloors to installing custom cabinetry — we've done it all, and we've done it well.

Our mission is to redefine what it means to work with a remodeling contractor. We don't just build spaces — we build relationships with our clients, with fellow tradesmen, and with the community we serve. Every project is an opportunity to prove that quality, professionalism, and trust still matter.

At the heart of our approach is a commitment to collaboration. We begin every project with a free consultation and a 3D rendering of your remodeled space — so you can see exactly what we're building before we break ground. Throughout construction, we provide regular updates on progress, timelines, and any changes. We use only top-quality materials and equipment, and every build follows current inspection codes. Because your space should be as safe and durable as it is beautiful.

We are building a network of trusted professionals across Monroe County and the surrounding areas — electricians, plumbers, HVAC technicians, finish carpenters, and specialty trades — who share our vision of superior quality and professional service. When you hire Construction Pioneers, you're not just hiring a contractor. You're hiring a team that cares.
